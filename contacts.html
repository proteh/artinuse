About us

Art in Use has been proudly serving the GTA area for over 10 years. Our excellent craftsmanship along with our high standards of customer service puts us at the top of our trade. We pride ourselves in offering the best resurfacing methods on porches, patios, pools, walkways and other areas with a uniqueness to each project that sets us apart.
With an almost unlimited combination of colours, even the most discerning of decorators have a multitude of choices to explore.

Twenty years on, these systems have been eagerly embraced by business owners, designers and architects, who demand a durable finish that effectively emulates the subtle nuances of stone, slate, marble and granite. Discover art in concrete and let your imagination run free.
