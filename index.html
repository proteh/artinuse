About us

Art in Use has been proudly serving Toronto and area for over 10 years. With experience dealing in organic shape designs and sculpture projects, we offer a uniqueness to each client that you will not find anywhere else.

We cover concrete, tile, brick, drywall and wood stone finishes. We also pride ourselves on offering the best resurfacing methods on porches, patios, walkways and pool areas.

Our team will provide you with custom design and color matching services for each project we work on. Our high craftsmanship puts us at the top of our trade.
